About us

Lazzaz Horse Riding Club is a Saudi based club bringing together like-minded equine enthusiasts of all levels for fun social and riding events. We offer a wide variety of riding for members including weekly lessons, weekends away, holidays and unique member only riding experiences from side saddle to advanced dressage training. We also have a very active social calendar. Meeting people with similar interests and making new friends is one of the major benefits of becoming a Riding Club member!
